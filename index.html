ABOUT THE ORGANISATION

Key features of our Organisation

Admission Under RTE Act

The RTE act mandates all the private schools to reserve 25% of the seats for children who come from economically weaker sections. Kshitij Organisation focus on talented students to help them get admitted in private schools under RTE Act.

Vidyadaan Scheme

Vidyadaan is an initiative by Gwalior District Administration aimed at enhancing the quality of educations that students at government schools receive. Industry specialists, professionals, administrative officers and educated people from different realms of society can take up this voluntary task. There is neither a restriction nor a minimum commitment needed for this self driven campaign.

Orientation

Underprivileged children lose their right to emotional, physical and social development and are more likely to have depression, low self esteem, lack of sleep and nutrition etc. Orientation programmes in kshitij includes health hygiene, menstruation problems, career guidance, physical fitness etc.

Activities

Activity-based learning is indulging students in activities by stimulating their senses, such as sight, smell, vision or feeling, and getting them involved in the subject.This method is much better and interesting than conventional method of learning.

We at Kshitij don't want any stone unturned for the betterment and upliftment of children. We are ready to give them what we got and pledge to make them capable enough, Whatever it takes! At Kshitij Education and Welfare Organisation, we believe that the gift of education is one of the biggest gift you can give an underprivileged. Not every kid gets the opportunity to study. Not every child can get over the family problems and pay attention in school, which again, may not have proper facilities. Moreover, the efforts of simply putting kids into schools doesn't guarantee quality education.
The goal of the NGO is to conceptualise ‘Quality Education for All’. The NGO nurtures the individual by imparting what is beyond the curriculum, by widening the imagination and watching the children as they bloom as special creations of the global society, as is well stated in our vision.
The basic ideas if our group includes -
 1. Providing free educational support to those students who are capable of doing wonders in their lives, but aren't able.to because of lack of resources. We will try to provide them proper grooming and a good platform to develop their personality.
2. To identify children who are poor and are not getting educated or have not enrolled in schools. Also, help administration to provide education to all.
